DISTANCE PLEASE

Science Carnival

Hello young scientists are you ready to show your creative ideas and solve problems in the society, then here is a golden oppurtunity submit your ideas and grab cash prizes and certificate of excellence

Eligibility:

PUC/Intermediate students only


Prize money: INR 10,000


  • description
  • guidelines
  • contact

Description: Participants should apply principles of science and mathematics to develop economical solutions to technical problems. They are expected to provide solutions for commercial and scientific applications that could meet the consumer and societal needs.

Teams: Participation individually or in a group with maximum limit of 4 members, of any college and any year. Teckzite registration is mandatory for every team member.

First Round: Screening Round

1.Prepare an abstract and send it to our mail address sciencecarnival@teckzite.org

2.Abstract must be written in English and shouldn’t exceed 300 words

3.Plagiarized abstracts/ideas will be disqualified

4.Abstract should specify the overall purpose of the projectandthe problem that you’ve investigated along with the proposed solution.

5.You should mention your team members names and TZ ID’s along with their details.

6. False identity will lead to disqualification

7. Please follow the time schedules meticulously

8. This will be an elimination round and the team which is qualified for second round will be informed through mail/call.


Deadline for video submission: 7th June, 2021. (6:00pm)

Second Round: Screening for finalists

1.You should record a short video that describes your project

2.Duration of the video shouldn’t exceed five minutes

3.Upload the it as unlisted video to YouTube (For your reference click here)

4.Send the YouTube link to our mail address ScienceCarnival2021@teckzite.org

5.You can also attach your journal/paper along with the video link but it isn’t mandatory.

6. Finalists will be announced based on the results of second round.


Deadline for video submission: 7th June, 2021. (6:00pm)

Final Round: Virtual Expo

1.Platform -Google meet

2.You should present your project with prototype before a panel

3.You’re free to choose the way you present

4.Time allotted for each project is 15 minutes

5.Your project will be validated based on the judgement criteria

6. In case of poor or unavailability of internet connection, please inform coordinators as soon as possible.

7.Punctuality, time management and demonstration skills will also be considered for judgement.

8. Based on the judging criteria winner team will be announced after the final round.

9.visit FAQ page for more details and only call the coordinators if you have ambiguity in the information provided.

10.Dont ask the coordinators and organizers for the ideas, search on internet and add in your own creativity.


Note: Project with working prototypes will have more chances of winning.


The discretion of coordinators is final and binding.
