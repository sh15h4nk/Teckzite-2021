teckzite

Unlock your skills

Teckzite, an annual National Level Techno-management fest celebrated and lionized by the student community of Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, Nuzvid.Teckzite excites the students by all means and makes them wait throughout the year.


Along with the ISO certification 45+ internships and placement offers are also provided to the winners.The main objective of the Teckzite is to stay fresh, exciting year after year and tweaking the challenges that the participants have to do. Every year around 7000+ students from 65+ colleges and universities take part in competitions and events of Teckzite
